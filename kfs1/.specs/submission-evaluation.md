# Turn-in and Peer-Evaluation

How the project is submitted and evaluated (chapter VI).

- Turn your work into your **Git repository**, as usual. Only the work present
  on your repository will be graded in defense.
- You must turn in:
  - your **code**,
  - a **Makefile**,
  - a **basic virtual image** for your kernel.
- ⚠ Be careful with that image: the kernel does nothing with it yet, so there is
  no need for it to be huge. **10 MB is the upper bound** — deal with it.
